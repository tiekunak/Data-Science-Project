Business and data understanding¶

The dataset collected from two websites through web crawling. The first set of data collected are headlines from a sports article and to be specific a football (soccer) article website "https://theconversation.com/us/topics/soccer-481?page=1/". The second set of data are titles collected from a human resource article website "https://www.hrdive.com/?page=1". Our task is to train our model to learn to identify football (Soccer) related articles and be able to differentiate these articles from other contents or articles automatically. Then, the predictive model will be used to classify a set of unknown (or unlabeled) articles as either football articles or not. Our first hypothesis is to expect sports or soccer articles to have words such as football, goal, player, names of plays¶
